Built for the Intelligent Web

Every feature designed to protect, empower, and simplify your browsing experience.

👻

Ghost Mode

Advanced fingerprint protection, automatic user-agent rotation, canvas noise injection, and WebGL spoofing. Browse without leaving digital traces.

🛡️

Scam Shield

Real-time fraud detection analyzing domain patterns, TLD reputation, brand impersonation attempts, and homograph attacks. Every site gets a risk score.

🔍

Smart Search

Multi-engine search with instant suggestions. Switch between DuckDuckGo, Google, Bing, and Startpage. Results are cached locally for instant recall.

🔒

Local-First Storage

All your bookmarks, history, preferences, and search cache stored exclusively on your device. No cloud sync means no data exposure.

🤖

WAB Agent Integration

Built-in Web Agent Bridge protocol support. Your agents work natively inside the browser, understanding every page visually and semantically.

🚫

Ad Blocker

Blocks 80+ ad networks, trackers, and popups at the network level. Cosmetic filtering hides leftover ad containers. Zero ads, zero tracking.

⚖️

Fairness System

Prioritizes small trusted websites over big tech monopolies. Analyzes domain trust, content quality, and independence to rank sites fairly.

📱

Mobile PWA

Install directly on your phone as a Progressive Web App. No app store needed. Full browser experience with ad blocking and agent chat on mobile.

⚡

Lightweight & Fast

Minimal footprint, no bloatware, no telemetry. Built on Chromium with only the essentials. Starts in seconds, stays fast forever.

How It Works

Three layers of intelligence working together to protect you.

1

You Browse

Navigate the web normally. Tabs, bookmarks, search — everything you expect from a browser, refined and simplified.

2

Shield Analyzes

Every page is silently analyzed for fraud patterns, suspicious domains, and scam indicators. You see a real-time safety score.

3

Ghost Protects

When Ghost Mode is on, your digital fingerprint is randomized. Sites cannot track, identify, or profile you across the web.

🔐 Your Data, Your Device

WAB Browser was built with a radical privacy philosophy: if data doesn't need to leave your device, it stays on your device.

  • All bookmarks and history stored locally only
  • Search cache stored on your device, never on our servers
  • No telemetry, no analytics, no tracking
  • Ghost Mode removes all digital fingerprints
  • DNT and GPC headers sent by default
  • WAB Account is optional — for platform features only
  • Open protocol, transparent behavior
